--------------------------------------------ZORKUL--------------------------------------------

Win Condition: Collect the four pieces of the Key.
Loss Condition: Take too much damage or lose too much stamina from travelling.

North, East, South, West buttons move players to the room in that direction.
Teleport button teleports player to a random room in the dungeon.
If an Item is found, The Player will type the name of the item in the grey box in the lower right corner and then click the Pick Up button.
This item will then be added to The Players inventory, if typed correctly.

The Player will lose Stamina when they press a directional button for travel.
The Player will lose Health if they ecounter an enemy on their travels between rooms.
